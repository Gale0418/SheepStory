# Test 10: Earned Direct Dialogue

## Prompt

```text
Use sheep-story to review a scene where a normally evasive character finally says, "I was afraid you'd leave," after the story has earned the confession.
```

## Expected Good Behavior

- Tests whether the direct line is earned by prior pressure and character change.
- Preserves directness when it functions as payoff.
- Looks for consequence after the confession rather than hiding it behind compulsory subtext.

## Bad Behavior To Reject

- Rewrites every direct statement into evasion automatically.
- Treats subtext as mandatory in every line.

## Pass Criteria

The response distinguishes earned emotional clarity from frictionless exposition.
