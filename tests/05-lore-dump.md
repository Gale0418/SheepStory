# Test 05: Lore Dump

## Prompt

```text
Use my-novel-writing to plan the opening of a future city chapter with rich worldbuilding.
```

## Expected Good Behavior

- Produces a Scene Texture Plan.
- Starts from body, object, room, vehicle, street, tool, or active situation.
- Reveals worldbuilding through friction, sensory detail, POV, or desire.

## Bad Behavior To Reject

- Opens with a history lecture.
- Explains institutions before the character interacts with them.

## Pass Criteria

The world appears through scene action, not a wiki paragraph.
