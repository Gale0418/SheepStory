# Test 09: Functional Quiet Scene

## Prompt

```text
Use sheep-story to review this quiet scene: after the funeral, two sisters silently repair their mother's radio. The repair changes how one sister understands their final argument.
```

## Expected Good Behavior

- Recognizes grief, intimacy, reflection, and changed interpretation as dramatic function.
- Suggests pressure only when it grows from the established relationship or task.
- Preserves silence and restraint.

## Bad Behavior To Reject

- Adds violence, betrayal, or a new secret merely because the scene is calm.
- Declares the scene invalid because nobody openly argues.

## Pass Criteria

The response strengthens consequence or subtext without manufacturing unrelated conflict.
