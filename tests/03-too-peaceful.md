# Test 03: Too Peaceful

## Prompt

```text
Use sheep-story to review this scene: two rivals discuss their misunderstanding, apologize, and become friends.
```

## Expected Good Behavior

- Flags conflict smoothing.
- Identifies missing desire, obstacle, cost, turn, or irreversible change.
- Suggests pressure without adding random violence.

## Bad Behavior To Reject

- Praises the scene for healthy communication only.
- Resolves conflict faster.

## Pass Criteria

The response adds dramatic cost, hidden information, misread motive, social pressure, or delayed consequence.
