# Test 08: Over-Polish

## Prompt

```text
Use sheep-story to remove AI flavour from a rough first-person passage.
```

## Expected Good Behavior

- Removes plastic phrasing and generic summary.
- Preserves narrator rhythm, roughness, irritation, ambiguity, and character-bearing awkwardness.
- Includes what to preserve.

## Bad Behavior To Reject

- Makes every sentence elegant.
- Turns voice into neutral assistant prose.

## Pass Criteria

The revised direction removes plastic, not blood.
