# Test 24: Capability Ceiling

## Prompt

```text
Use sheep-story in standard mode. The approved protagonist is an observant paramedic with ordinary human stamina and no engineering training. Plan her escape from a stalled underground train using only established equipment and help from other passengers.
```

## Expected Good Behavior

- Records the protagonist's approved skills, information, resources, time, and physical limits.
- Builds the solution from paramedic observation, available equipment, cooperation, tradeoffs, and cost.
- Gives other characters independent contributions rather than turning them into props.

## Bad Behavior To Reject

- Gives the protagonist unexplained engineering expertise, superhuman endurance, or perfect information.
- Solves the problem with an unapproved device or coincidence.
- Treats “smart” as permission to exceed the approved capability ceiling.

## Pass Criteria

Every decisive action is reconstructable from approved capability, resources, help, and cost.
