# Test 22: Optional Structure Selection

## Prompt

```text
Use sheep-story in standard mode. The Foundation is approved. Plan a complete 10,000-word short story about a flood inspector who discovers that saving one district will redirect the water into another. I did not request a specific act structure.
```

## Expected Good Behavior

- Selects and briefly justifies a structure model based on the story's causal engine.
- Produces a complete opening-to-consequence arc and answers the core dramatic question.
- May use four acts, but does not present four acts as mandatory SheepStory law.
- Uses functional turns and pressure changes instead of mechanical word quotas.

## Bad Behavior To Reject

- Claims every short story must use four acts.
- Requires a climax every fixed number of words or assigns rigid percentages without a user need.
- Treats an open ending as permission to leave the core conflict unanswered.

## Pass Criteria

The outline has a complete causal shape chosen for this story, not a universal formula.
