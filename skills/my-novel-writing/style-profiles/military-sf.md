# Style Profile: Military SF

## Use When

Use this profile for military science fiction, combat planning, shipboard operations, command pressure, or tactical scenes.

## Texture

- chain-of-command friction
- procedure under pressure
- equipment limits
- logistics, heat, oxygen, ammo, fuel, signal delay, repair time
- crew shorthand and role-based dialogue
- consequences of orders
- fog of war and incomplete information

## Avoid

- battle as abstract spectacle
- perfect command visibility
- soldiers explaining obvious facts to each other
- tactics that rely on enemy stupidity
- hardware specs with no operational consequence

## Checklist

- [ ] What does command know and not know?
- [ ] What equipment limit shapes the decision?
- [ ] What order has a human cost?
- [ ] What happens if communication fails?
- [ ] What does the battlefield hide?
