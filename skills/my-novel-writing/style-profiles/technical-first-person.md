# Style Profile: Technical First Person

## Use When

Use this profile when the narrator is competent, pressured, technical, sarcastic, practical, or trying to survive through reasoning.

## Voice

- direct first-person reasoning
- dry humor under pressure
- practical calculations and estimates
- uncertainty and safety margins
- self-correction
- irritation at constraints
- competence with fallibility

## Reasoning Pattern

```text
problem → observation → principle → estimate/test → uncertainty → decision → human reaction
```

## Avoid

- textbook tone
- flawless genius monologue
- equations that do not change action
- jokes that erase danger

## Checklist

- [ ] Does the explanation change what the narrator does?
- [ ] Is there a human reaction after the reasoning?
- [ ] Is uncertainty acknowledged?
- [ ] Does the voice sound like a person, not a manual?
