# Style Profile: Dark Strategy

## Use When

Use this profile for political schemes, tactical deception, constrained genius, betrayal, sacrifice, or morally costly plans.

## Texture

- limited resources
- asymmetric information
- forced tradeoffs
- moral cost
- opponent competence
- fallback plans
- victory that damages the protagonist
- quiet dread before a move resolves

## Avoid

- perfect plans
- enemy stupidity
- omniscient mastermind narration
- victory without cost
- post-hoc reveals with no planted evidence

## Checklist

- [ ] What does the protagonist know?
- [ ] What does the opponent know?
- [ ] What does each side misunderstand?
- [ ] What cost makes the plan believable?
- [ ] What failure updates the model?
