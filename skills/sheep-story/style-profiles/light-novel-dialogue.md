# Style Profile: Light Novel Dialogue

## Use When

Use this profile when the user wants fast character interaction, banter, emotional contrast, or readable scene flow.

## Texture

- clear speaker rhythm
- quick turns
- subtext under jokes
- strong character contrast
- small physical beats between lines
- comedy that exposes tension
- emotional pivots hidden under casual speech

## Avoid

- every line sounding like exposition
- everyone being too polite
- joke chains with no story movement
- dialogue without intention or obstacle

## Checklist

- [ ] What does each speaker want?
- [ ] Who blocks whom?
- [ ] What is hidden under the joke?
- [ ] Could the lines be swapped between characters? If yes, revise.
