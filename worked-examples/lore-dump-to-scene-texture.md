# Worked Example: Lore Dump → Scene Texture

## Bad Pattern

```text
The city was governed by a centralized transit authority founded after the orbital war. It had three transit layers and strict identity controls.
```

## Why It Fails

- starts outside the scene
- explains institution before consequence
- no body, object, friction, or desire
- could be pasted into a wiki

## Better Direction

Start with a character trying to move through the city.

```text
She pressed her wristband to the gate. The gate blinked, thought about it, then politely refused her existence.

Above her, the upper tram slid past with all the people whose permissions had updated on time. Below the platform, the freight layer groaned open and exhaled warm machine air into her face.
```

## What Improved

- transit layers appear through action
- identity control becomes friction
- the city has sound, heat, and vertical space
- the character has a problem

## Checklist

- [ ] Does the world enter through action?
- [ ] Does the setting create friction?
- [ ] Does the detail affect the character's next move?
