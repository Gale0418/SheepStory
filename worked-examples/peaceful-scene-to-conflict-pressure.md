# Worked Example: Peaceful Scene → Conflict Pressure

## Bad Pattern

```text
They talked honestly, understood each other, apologized, and agreed to work together.
```

## Why It Fails

- no cost
- no obstacle
- no irreversible change
- no future pressure

## Better Direction

Let cooperation create a new wound.

```text
“I can get you through the checkpoint,” Mara said.

“That sounds like help.”

“It is help.” She set the badge on the table, face down. “It also means my sister's name stays out of your report.”

He looked at the badge. Then at the locked clinic door behind her.

There were three people bleeding inside and one rule left between him and treason.

He picked up the badge.
```

## What Improved

- cooperation has a price
- decision creates moral debt
- relationship changes under pressure
- the story cannot reset after the scene

## Checklist

- [ ] What does the agreement cost?
- [ ] Who loses leverage?
- [ ] What consequence follows?
